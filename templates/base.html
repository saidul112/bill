❓ How to Use Bill Splitter
📚 Overview

Bill Splitter helps roommates track shared expenses and calculate who owes what. The app makes it easy to manage utility bills, split costs fairly, and account for different usage patterns.

🏠 Step 1: Creating a Profile

What is a Profile? A profile represents your household or shared living space.

  • Click "Create New Profile" from the dashboard
  • Enter a name for your household (e.g., "Apartment 302")
  • Add all roommates who share expenses
  • Each profile can have multiple monthly billing periods
👥 Managing Roommates

You can add or remove roommates at any time:

  • Click the "+" button next to the roommate list
  • Enter the roommate's name and click "Add"
  • To remove someone, click the "×" next to their name
  • Changes apply to future calculations, not past months
📅 Step 2: Monthly Billing Periods

What is a Month? Each month is a separate billing period where you track expenses.

  • From your profile page, click "Create New Month"
  • Enter the month name (e.g., "January 2024")
  • Set the start and end dates for the billing period
  • You can have multiple active months and view historical data
💰 Step 3: Adding Bills

How to add a bill:

  1. Open a monthly period and click "+ Bill" under Quick Actions
  2. Select the Bill Type: Gas, Electricity, Water, or Extras
  3. For Extras, you can add a sub-type (e.g., "Toilet Paper", "Cleaning Supplies")
  4. Enter the Amount in your currency
  5. Set the Date Range (from date → to date) that this bill covers
  6. Check "Already paid" if someone has paid this bill
  7. Select who paid from the dropdown
  8. Add an optional description for notes

💡 Tip: Bills are automatically split equally among all active roommates by default.

⊘ Excluding Members from Bills

Sometimes a bill shouldn't be shared by everyone:

  • When adding/editing a bill, scroll to "Exclude Members"
  • Check the boxes for roommates who should NOT pay for this bill
  • The bill will be split only among the remaining roommates
  • Use cases: Personal purchases, items used by only some people

Example: If you buy toilet paper but one roommate provides their own, exclude them from that Extras bill.

⚙️ Usage Adjustments (Absences)

What are adjustments? If someone was away (vacation, business trip, etc.), they shouldn't pay full price for utilities.

How to add an adjustment:

  1. Click "⚙️ Adjustment" under Quick Actions
  2. Select the roommate who was absent
  3. Choose which bill type to adjust (usually Gas, Electricity, Water)
  4. Set the absence dates (from → to)
  5. Add a reason (e.g., "Vacation in Bali")

💡 How it works: If the bill period is 30 days and someone was away 10 days, they only pay for 20/30 (66.7%) of their share.

Example: 3 roommates, Gas bill is ¥9,000 for 30 days. Normally each pays ¥3,000. If one person was away 15 days, they pay ¥1,500 (15/30 × ¥3,000), and the other two split the remaining ¥7,500.

🧮 How Calculations Work

The app automatically calculates fair splits using this logic:

  1. Base Split: Each bill is divided equally among all roommates (minus excluded members)
  2. Adjustments Applied: If someone has an absence, their share is reduced proportionally based on days away
  3. Redistribution: The reduced amount is redistributed to other roommates
  4. Payment Tracking: The system tracks who paid what
  5. Final Balance: Your balance = (What you paid) - (What you owe)
Formula for adjustments:
Adjusted Share = Normal Share × (Days Present / Total Days in Bill Period)
📊 Understanding the Payment Summary

The summary shows the financial breakdown for each person:

  • "Gets ¥X.XX" (Green) = This person overpaid and should receive money back
  • "Owes ¥X.XX" (Red) = This person needs to pay this amount
  • "✓ Settled" (Gray) = This person is all paid up

Details shown:

  • Bills: Total amount this person owes based on their usage
  • Paid: Total amount this person has paid
  • Balance: The difference (Paid - Owed)

💡 The system verifies that Total Bills = Total Split amount. If there's a mismatch, it shows a warning.

✏️ Editing and Deleting
  • Click the ✏️ (pencil) icon on any bill or adjustment to edit it
  • Click the 🗑️ (trash) icon to delete
  • Changes recalculate balances automatically
💡 Tips & Best Practices
  • 📅 Create months early: Set up next month before bills arrive
  • 📸 Track receipts: Use the description field to note receipt numbers
  • ⚖️ Regular check-ins: Review balances weekly to stay on top of payments
  • 💬 Communicate: Discuss large expenses before adding them
  • 🔄 Settle up monthly: Transfer money at month-end to keep balances clear
  • 📱 Use on mobile: Add bills right after shopping using your phone
❓ Common Questions

Q: Can I change who paid a bill after adding it?

A: Yes! Click the edit icon (✏️) and change the "Paid By" field.

Q: What if someone moves in mid-month?

A: Add them as a roommate, then exclude them from bills before they arrived, or add adjustments for their absence.

Q: Can I split a bill unequally?

A: Currently bills split equally, but you can use "Exclude Members" to control who pays, or add adjustments for absences.

Q: How do I handle one-time shared purchases?

A: Use "Extras" bill type with a descriptive sub-type and the date of purchase.
